About me

Experienced Certified Financial Education Instructor and Investment Professional in Ghana with a decade of expertise. Currently a Senior Associate at Temple Investments managing a $3.5M portfolio, contributing to GHS30 billion in capital market transactions.

Founder of Investment_friend, an Instagram platform empowering over 4500 predominantly female followers with tailored financial literacy content and coaching. Previous success at First Atlantic Asset Management includes outperforming benchmarks and leading portfolio restructuring.

Holds an MSc. in Industrial Finance & Investment and demonstrates a commitment to ongoing professional development. Passionate about driving financial literacy, particularly among African women, and dedicated to fostering positive change through strategic investment management.
